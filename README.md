# Dynamics

Selected materials from the Dynamics course at Universidad de los Andes. This
repository is organized as a university course archive, not as a semester
portfolio.

Dynamics is one of my strongest areas of academic interest. At Universidad de
los Andes, this course is a CPO (Professional Cycle Elective).

## Structure

- `code/`: reproducible notebooks and scripts.
- `datasets/`: required inputs and selected Workshop 2 documents.
- `archive/`: complementary submissions and final documents.

## Highlights

- `code/Taller 2.ipynb`: kinematics, spline trajectories, inverse dynamics, and
  spring selection.
- `code/taller2_mujoco_common.py`: shared geometry, trajectories, and MuJoCo
  scene for Workshop 2.
- `code/Taller 3.ipynb`: mechanism development for Workshop 3.
- `code/taller3_pipeline.py`: pipeline for regenerating Workshop 3 results and
  visualizations.
- `code/analyze_servo_current.py`: comparative current analysis with and without
  a spring.
- `code/volante_inercia_6061.ipynb`: flywheel calculations.

## Requirements

Python 3.9 or later with `numpy`, `scipy`, `sympy`, `mujoco`, `ipykernel`,
`nbclient`, and `Pillow`.

## Notes

Original course artifacts may retain their submitted filenames and language.
Generated GIFs, images, traces, and summaries are intentionally excluded.
